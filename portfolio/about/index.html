About Katie

Thanks for stopping by. Read below to learn more about my background and journey into product management.

Background

I'm a product manager with a unique blend of technical skills, educational expertise, and user-centered design experience. My journey has taken me from teaching in classrooms to designing digital learning products that serve thousands of users across multiple countries.

What drives me is the challenge of translating complex user needs into elegant product solutions. Whether it's designing an interactive learning platform for Pacific communities or transforming clinical education portfolios from paper to digital, I thrive on solving problems that make a real difference in people's lives.

Currently based in Nelson, New Zealand, I work remotely with distributed teams and have experience collaborating across time zones and cultures. I'm a Certified Scrum Master who believes in iterative development, data-driven decisions, and the power of cross-functional collaboration to build products that users love.
